About Harumanis Mangoes

Harumanis mangoes are celebrated for their exceptional sweetness and unique aroma. Grown exclusively in the state of Perlis, they are known for their premium quality.

UiTM Perlis has been instrumental in advancing research and techniques for growing these mangoes, ensuring sustainable farming methods and supporting local farmers.

Health Benefits

  • Rich in Vitamin C and antioxidants.
  • Promotes digestive health and boosts immunity.
  • Supports skin health with essential nutrients.

Culinary Uses

  • Perfect for desserts, smoothies, and beverages.
  • Used in jams, ice creams, and other food products.
  • Make vibrant dishes like Mango Acar and Mango Pudding.
